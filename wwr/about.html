Purpose: To empower thrill-seekers to connect with nature and challenge themselves through unforgettable water rafting experiences."

Creed: We pledge to: - Safeguard our guests and the environment - Deliver exceptional service and expertise - Foster a culture of adventure and camaraderie - Continuously innovate and improve
Motto: "Ride the Rapids, Live the Adventure"

HISTORY

It was Founded in 1985 by avid outdoorsman and rafting enthusiast, John Taylor, Rapids has established itself as a premier rafting company. Starting with a small fleet of meticulously maintained rafts and a team of highly experienced guides, Taylor's vision was to provide unparalleled whitewater experiences. Taylor's passion for rafting and commitment to safety, expertise, and customer satisfaction quickly resonated with adventurers seeking thrilling, yet secure, excursions. Word of Rapids' exceptional service spread, attracting a loyal client base and fueling steady growth.
